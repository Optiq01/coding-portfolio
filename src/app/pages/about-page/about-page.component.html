Why I Code

My passion for coding began with a desire to build web platforms for my various creative endeavors. Due to the highly custom nature of my ideas these platforms required a level of complexity and functionality that made them more "software" like than what I initially anticipated. After trying different CMS platforms and other shop builders to no avail I decided to dive into full stack development to become skilled enough to emulate my face-to-face operations in code.

My Preferred Framework

I've grown quite fond of Angular and have been using it as my go to platform for building my ideas. I love React and Vue as well however I noticed the larger my applications became the more I would default to doing things "the Angular way" to keep everything concise, organized and accessible where needed. I've come to appreciate the strictness of Typescript because as a project grows, I can only think so much at one time and the TS compiler reminds me of the things I should be thinking about that I might not be considering in the moment.
